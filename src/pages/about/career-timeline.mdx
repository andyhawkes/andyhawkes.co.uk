# My career

My career to date has been completely unplanned and generally guided by a mixture of fate, coincidence, impulsive decisions, and good luck, so it's not really in my modus operandi to plan my career moves ahead of time.

I have been creating websites and working with "the internet" in one way or another for over 30 years - at first I did it for fun, then I got paid to do it, and now I get paid to direct and manage other people who manage people who do it much better than I can any more.
