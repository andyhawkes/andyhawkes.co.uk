# Consultancy pricing

My work varies based on:

* number of stakeholders
* organisational complexity
* ambiguity level

I am not pricing "a week of work" - I am pricing clarity that changes how the organisation operates.

## Diagnostic

High intensity, short duration.

### Lower end

£8k–£10k

* smaller org
* tighter scope
* fewer stakeholders
* cleaner system

### Upper end

£12k–£15k

* multiple teams
* unclear structure
* complex dependencies
* messy decision-making

## Derp

## Advisory

Advisory = access + continuity + leverage

I am providing:

* continuity of thinking
* pattern recognition over time
* fast course correction
* decision support

Not:

* output
* delivery
* capacity

### Practical advisory tiers

#### Tier 1 — Light advisory

£2k–£3k/month

Includes:

* 1–2 calls/month (60–90 mins)
* async access (Slack/email)
* occasional doc review

Positioning:

“Light-touch support to help you stay on track and avoid drifting back into the same issues”

#### Tier 2 — Active advisory

£3k–£5k/month

Includes:

* weekly or bi-weekly calls
* deeper involvement in decisions
* reviewing plans, structures, changes
* more active challenge

Positioning:

“Ongoing support while you’re actively making changes to the system”

#### Tier 3 — Embedded advisory

£5k–£8k/month

Only if:

* limited number of clients
* clearly bounded

Includes:

* regular involvement
* key decision support
* some presence in leadership cadence
